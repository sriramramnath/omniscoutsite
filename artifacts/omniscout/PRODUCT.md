# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Developers and technical teams evaluating local browser automation and web research for shell-capable AI agents such as Codex, Claude Code, Cursor, and Cline.

## Product Purpose

OmniScout gives AI agents a real, locally controlled browser and concise web-research outputs without hosted browser sessions or vendor lock-in. The site should make the product model understandable quickly and move visitors toward installation, documentation, comparison, or the source repository.

## Brand Personality

Technical, direct, premium. The experience should feel like a capable developer tool with confident product storytelling and carefully resolved interactions.

## Anti-references

Avoid crowded navigation, generic SaaS mega-menus, decorative glass effects, vague marketing copy, and interaction patterns that hide essential destinations or depend on pointer hover alone.

## Design Principles

- Make the local-first product model immediately legible.
- Reduce navigation choices by grouping destinations around visitor intent.
- Show real product context instead of relying on abstract decoration.
- Keep calls to action direct and technically credible.
- Make polished pointer interactions equally usable by keyboard and touch.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and interaction behavior. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus, touch-friendly controls, reduced motion, and dropdowns that remain operable without hover.
